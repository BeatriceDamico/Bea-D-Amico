App- notes

Objectives: a virtual notebook that fits in your pocket and allows users to jot down thoughts, memories, personal objectives, and all other info one would write in a notebook.


Audience: mostly all ages
10-25: mostly students and teens who jot down notes for school, or personal thoughts for themselves
25-on: workers, and all other humans who feel forgetful and need to write something down. etc

-People who don’t want to waste paper use notes. -People who don’t have space in their bag for a notebook/find notebooks pointless with technology. -People who like the idea of keeping notes in their pocket (technically it is pocket size)

Needs: a place that the user can depend on to go back to their past writing and recall all the thoughts and plans/ideas/etc that the user wrote.


Scope of Notes Can save to the icloud -user can keep it forever in the cloud if it is very important Can create a new note rather than have one page User can have multiple notes like an actual notebook experience Can edit Users can go back and “erase” past writing or write new notes Everytime you write on a note whether it is new or old, it goes back to the top User is reminded of most recents notes just in case they need to write in it again. As a writer I want to write my thoughts So i can remember them in the future.
